PHINMA-Araullo University

PHINMA Araullo University is a tertiary educational institution with campuses located in Cabanatuan in Nueva Ecija that offers quality and affordable education. What started as a simple law school in 1950 is now a major educational institution that provides basic, tertiary education and professional studies to Novo Ecijanos

PHINMA AU provides quality education at affordable rates and offer scholarships in order to give more students the chance to expand their horizons for their future.

We provide guidance to each and every student, developing their necessary knowledge and skills and forming their character and spirit every step of the way. Through our student-centric and communal mentorship approach, we ensure that they work hard and succeed in all their endeavors in their academic journey. In PHINMA AU, we make sure that no student is left behind.
